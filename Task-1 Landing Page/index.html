Destinations


London

Experience the vibrant culture and history of London, from the iconic Big Ben and Tower of London to the trendy neighborhoods of Shoreditch and Camden. Take a stroll along the South Bank, see a show in the West End, or shop on Oxford Street. London offers something for everyone, whether you're interested in art, music, theatre, or fashion.

Paris

Fall in love with the City of Light, from the iconic Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum to the charming cafés and boutiques of Montmartre. Take a romantic stroll along the Seine, visit the famous Notre Dame Cathedral, or indulge in some of the world's best food and wine. Paris is a city of art, culture, and romance.

Book Now
New York

Discover the energy and diversity of New York City, from the towering skyscrapers of Manhattan to the colorful street art and delicious food of Brooklyn. Take a ferry to the Statue of Liberty, catch a Broadway show, or shop in Times Square. New York is a melting pot of cultures and is known for its iconic landmarks, world-class museums and excellent shopping.
